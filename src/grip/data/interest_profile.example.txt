## Core Research Areas
AI applications to medical imaging, including image reconstruction, automated diagnosis,
image simulation, guided acquisition, and neural representations. Primary methods:
generative modeling (diffusion models, flow matching) and neural representations (NeRFs).
Focus modalities: cardiac imaging and ultrasound; broadly interested in other imaging
modalities and sensor systems.

## Adjacent Areas of Interest
Robotics and medical robotics, multimodal language models, protein structure prediction,
and ML theory for generative models.

## Explicit Exclusions
Pure NLP research without medical or imaging relevance.
